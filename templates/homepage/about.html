About ISRO VIMARSH

What is ISRO VIMARSH?

ISRO VIMARSH is a fan-made website dedicated to showcasing the incredible achievements and missions of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Our Mission

To create an enthusiastic and modern platform where space enthusiasts around the world can learn about ISRO's rockets, missions, and contributions to space exploration.

Features

  • Comprehensive rocket database with specifications
  • Latest news about ISRO missions and achievements
  • Historical timeline of ISRO's journey
  • Mission details and highlights

Disclaimer

This is an unofficial fan website and is not affiliated with or endorsed by ISRO. All information is collected from public sources and presented for educational purposes.
